Competing Campaigns — Paseo Testnet

100 campaigns are seeded on Paseo testnet with CPM bids ranging from 0.300–0.700 PAS. Each campaign targets a random subset of the 30-topic taxonomy (including Polkadot-native topics like DeFi, NFTs, Polkadot Ecosystem, and DAOs), with ~20% carrying a USDT native asset sidecar reward per impression. The Datum extension runs a Vickrey (second-price) auction on every page visit — effectiveBid = CPM × interestWeight, so a lower-CPM campaign with a matching topic tag routinely beats a higher raw bid from an unmatched advertiser.

All 100 campaigns are active and competing simultaneously. Interest weight from your browse history is multiplied into each effective bid — a 0.30 PAS campaign with a strong topic match can beat a 0.70 PAS untagged campaign when your profile aligns. Sidecar USDT rewards are credited to user wallets via DatumTokenRewardVault regardless of auction outcome — they do not affect CPM ordering. Use the auto-tour to cycle all 30 topics and watch the winning campaign change in real time.
