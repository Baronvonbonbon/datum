Competing Campaigns — Paseo Testnet

Four campaigns are seeded on Paseo testnet at staggered CPM bids. The Datum extension runs a Vickrey (second-price) auction on each page visit — effective bid = CPM × interest weight. A lower-CPM campaign with a matching topic tag can beat a higher raw bid from an unmatched campaign. Visit matching topics to watch the outcome change in the auction monitor below.
