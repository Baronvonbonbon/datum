## Per-user fee burn (annual)

Annual transaction-fee cost (DOT/PAS) for a single end user across four
engagement tiers. Excludes posted bonds, locked stake, and DATUM token movement —
pure gas spend. Calculation: `Σ ops × gas[op] × 365 × gas_price`.

### Tier definitions

| Tier | Description | Onboard/yr | Reports/yr | Withdrawals/yr |
|---|---|---:|---:|---:|
| Minimal | Signs up once, never reports, withdraws once per year. | 1.0 | 0 | 1 |
| Typical | Default engagement: yearly onboarding, monthly report + withdraw. | 1.0 | 12 | 12 |
| Active | Engaged user: weekly report, fortnightly withdraw. | 1.0 | 52 | 24 |
| Power | Heavy user: bi-weekly stake adjustments, daily reports, weekly withdraw. | 26.1 | 365 | 52 |
