Ship a container to 30 edges with one command

A field guide to zero-config edge deploys that don't wake you up at 3am.

Edge platforms have quietly gotten good. The workflow that used to need a YAML thesis is now a single push, and the rollback is just as fast.

The trick is treating the edge like a CDN for compute: immutable artifacts, content-addressed builds, and health checks that fail closed.

We benchmarked cold starts across three providers; the spread was smaller than the variance inside any one region. Pick on ergonomics, not microbenchmarks.
