Weeknight miso-butter udon

Fifteen minutes, one pan, and a pantry you probably already have.

This is the dish to learn when you want something that tastes slow on a night that is anything but. Miso and butter do almost all the work.

Start the water before anything else. Thick udon cooks fast, and the sauce comes together in the time it takes to boil.

Finish with a runny egg and a fistful of scallions. The yolk is the sauce's better half.
